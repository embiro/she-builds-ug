Campaign Timeline

4 Weeks. Structured Execution.

Week 1

Launch & Ignite

NextMedia push goes live. Application form opens. Social campaign launches with #SheBuildsUG. Target: 8–10 signups. Daily outreach: 5 DMs + 2 posts.

Community Surge

Begin women's group visits (2–3/week). Mid-campaign check on lead quality. Boost top-performing posts. Target: 12–15 new signups. Daily outreach: 5 DMs + 2 posts + 1 group visit.

NextMedia Second Push + Close Applications

Second wave of media coverage. Share first applicant stories (build FOMO). Close application form. Begin shortlisting. Target: 12–15 new signups.

Week 4

Select, Announce & Begin Building

Announce the 50 selected businesses publicly. Onboarding calls begin. First projects assigned to developers. Post celebration content. Target: builds started on 20+ projects.

Selection Criteria

Who Gets Selected

✅ Must Meet All of These

  • ✓ Women-owned or women-led business
  • ✓ Active business (operating, not just an idea)
  • ✓ No existing professional website or app
  • ✓ Clear use case — can describe what the site/app needs to do
  • ✓ Able to provide content within 5 business days of selection
  • ✓ Based in Uganda

❌ Will Not Be Selected If

  • ✗ Already has a working website or app
  • ✗ Business is not active or is pre-revenue with no customers
  • ✗ Cannot provide own content (logo, text, photos)
  • ✗ Requesting features outside free scope (e.g. full e-commerce with payment integrations)
  • ✗ Already a paying Embiro client

Priority will be given to: Businesses in underserved industries (market vendors, tailors, small food producers), founders from upcountry Uganda, and nominees from trusted partner organizations (SACCOs, NGOs, unis).

Project Scope & Terms

What's Included. What's Not.

🌐 Free Website Includes:

  • ✓ Up to 5 pages (Home, About, Services, Gallery, Contact)
  • ✓ Mobile-responsive design
  • ✓ Custom branding (colors, logo, fonts)
  • ✓ Contact form + WhatsApp button
  • ✓ Basic SEO setup
  • ✓ 30-day post-launch support

Not included: domain/hosting (client purchases), ongoing maintenance, e-commerce/payments, custom integrations.

📲 Free MVP App Includes:

  • ✓ One core feature (e.g. product catalogue, booking, ordering)
  • ✓ Cross-platform (Android + iOS)
  • ✓ Custom branding
  • ✓ Basic user flow (view, browse, contact)
  • ✓ 30-day post-launch support

Not included: payment processing, backend admin panel, complex APIs, app store submission fees.

Client responsibility: Provide all content (logo, text, images) within 5 business days of selection. Projects that miss this deadline move to the waitlist.
